Summary

The visualization above incorporates the data described here (with some outliers removed).

Each bubble represents a particular wine, and each wine is located on the graph indicating its sulphate and acidity amounts measured in g/dm3 (i.e., grams per cubic decimeter).

Quality scores have been grouped for simplicity (low: 3 and 4, mid: 5 and 6, high: 7 and 8)
